Bookkeeping Cleanup & Catch-Up

Behind on your books? Bank feeds a disaster? Transactions from 2022 still sitting in "uncategorized"? We've seen it all, and we can fix it.

Cleanup isn't just about making QuickBooks look nice. It's about getting your numbers accurate so you can actually trust them. That means reconciling accounts, fixing miscategorized transactions, cleaning up duplicates, and making sure your reports reflect reality.

We'll get you caught up and set up so staying current is actually manageable going forward.

Monthly Bookkeeping

Ongoing bookkeeping that keeps your numbers current and your sanity intact. We handle the day-to-day so you can focus on running your business.

What this looks like: transaction categorization, bank and credit card reconciliation, accounts payable and receivable tracking, and monthly financial statements you can actually understand.

The goal isn't just accurate books - it's books that tell you something useful.

Tax Prep & Sales Tax

We get your books tax-ready so your CPA isn't starting from scratch. If you're handling sales tax across multiple states (especially you, e-commerce folks), we can manage the filings too.

We're not CPAs and we don't file income taxes, but we make sure everything is organized, categorized correctly, and reconciled before it goes to your tax preparer. That usually saves you money on their end too.

Payroll

Payroll setup, processing, and compliance. We make sure your people get paid correctly and on time, and that you're staying on the right side of state and federal requirements.

If you're running payroll through Gusto, QuickBooks Payroll, or another platform, we can manage it. If you're not sure what you should be using, we can help figure that out too.

E-commerce Accounting

Selling online comes with its own accounting headaches - inventory, COGS, multi-channel sales, platform fees, sales tax nexus. We get it.

For standard e-commerce bookkeeping, we've got you covered. For complex e-commerce operations that need specialized expertise, we partner with specialists who live and breathe this stuff. Either way, you're in good hands.

We work in your software. QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, FreshBooks, Zoho Books, Wave - whatever you're using, we can work with it. And if you're using the wrong tool for your business, we'll tell you.
