Business Systems & Automation

You're doing the same thing manually 50 times a week. Data entry. Sending the same emails. Copying information from one system to another. It's tedious and it's error-prone.

We set up automations that handle the repetitive stuff. Connect your calendar to your CRM. Auto-generate invoices when jobs close. Send follow-up emails without thinking about it.

The goal is getting you out of the weeds so you can focus on work that actually requires your brain.

SEO & Analytics Setup

You have a website. Is it actually bringing in business? Most small business owners have no idea because they've never set up proper tracking.

We set up analytics so you can see what's working. Where your traffic comes from. What pages people actually look at. Whether your marketing is doing anything.

For SEO, we focus on the basics that actually matter - making sure Google can find you when people search for what you do. Not magic tricks, just solid fundamentals.

Marketing Strategy

Marketing for small businesses is different than marketing for big companies. You don't have unlimited budget. You can't do everything. You need to pick the things that actually work for your situation.

We help you figure out where to focus. What channels make sense for your business. What messaging actually resonates. How to get more from what you're already doing.

This is strategy, not execution - though we can help you find the right people to execute if you need it.
