Fractional CFO & Strategic Finance

The high-level financial thinking your business needs - without the six-figure salary. Pricing strategy, profitability analysis, and financial modeling for real decisions.

Let's Talk Strategy

Profitability Analysis

You know you're making money (or you think you are), but do you know which services, customers, or jobs are actually profitable? Most business owners don't - and it's costing them.

We dig into your numbers and show you exactly where your profit is coming from - and where it's leaking out. Sometimes the answer is surprising. Often it changes how you run your business.

This isn't a generic report. It's a custom analysis of your specific situation, with recommendations you can actually act on.

Pricing Strategy & Restructuring

Most small businesses are undercharging. They set prices based on what competitors charge, what feels "fair," or what they've always charged - not on what the numbers actually support.

We help you figure out what you should be charging based on your real costs, your market, and your goals. Then we help you actually make the change - including how to communicate it to customers.

Raising prices is scary. We make it less scary by giving you the data to back it up.

Financial Modeling for Growth

Thinking about hiring? Adding a new service? Taking on a big contract? These decisions shouldn't be guesses.

We build financial models that show you what happens under different scenarios. What if you hire now vs. in six months? What if that big client pays late? What does "success" actually look like in dollars?

You get a tool you can use to test assumptions and make decisions with confidence.

Business Turnaround Support

Sometimes things are worse than "I need help with my books." Sometimes the business is in trouble and you need someone who can help you figure out what to do.

We've helped businesses go from zero revenue to $45K/month. We've helped companies restructure around new regulations. We've helped owners figure out whether to keep fighting or wind things down gracefully.

If you're in a tough spot, we can help you see it clearly and make a plan.

This isn't about replacing your accountant. Your bookkeeper keeps the books. Your CPA does your taxes. A fractional CFO helps you use that information to make better decisions about your business. We work alongside your existing team.
