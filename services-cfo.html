Beyond Bookkeeping

Bookkeeping tells you what happened. A CFO tells you what to do about it — and what's coming next.

You don't need a full-time CFO to get CFO-level thinking. You need someone who can look at your numbers, see the patterns, and help you make decisions with confidence instead of gut feel.

What We Do

  • Profitability Analysis — Which services, clients, and jobs actually make money? Which ones are quietly draining you?
  • Pricing Strategy — Are your rates right? How do you know? We model it.
  • Financial Modeling — What happens if you hire? Raise prices? Lose a client? We build the scenario so you can decide.
  • Cash Flow Planning — Revenue is great, but cash is king. We help you see what's coming.
  • Decision Support — Big decisions deserve real data. We help you find it.

Who This Is For

Service business owners who are past the startup phase but not ready for a full-time finance hire. You're making money, but you're not sure you're making the right decisions. You want a partner who can see the whole picture — not just the ledger.
