1 Year Anniversary

← Back to all posts

As we celebrate JNB's one-year anniversary, we can't help but reflect on how far we've come.

We started out with a simple goal: to help businesses with their bookkeeping and tax preparation.

But what a journey it has been!

Along the way, we've discovered that "I need help with my books" often means so much more.

It's become about guiding businesses through financial planning, data analysis, web development, SEO, and even marketing strategies. We've rebranded, grown our client base from three to fifteen, and built a fantastic team of passionate individuals.

This year has taught us that understanding the financials is just the beginning, and we're honored to help businesses thrive in every way we can.

Here's to year two

Thank you to every client who trusted us with their business. We're just getting started.
