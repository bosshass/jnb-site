Why I built this

I kept watching the same thing happen across every client: the owner runs dispatch from their truck. The office manager re-keys timesheets into QuickBooks. Nobody knows who owes what until they check three different systems.

Lifeline exists because I got tired of building the same workarounds for every client. It’s the platform I needed to exist — so I built it.

It connects your field to your financials. No more spreadsheets pretending to be systems. No more guessing if that job got billed. No more finding out you’re owed $40K at the end of the quarter.

Lifeline is your business’s vital signs — in real time.
