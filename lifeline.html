The Problem It Solves

You know the feeling. Someone asks you a simple question about your business — how much revenue this month, how much cash is available, whether you can afford a hire — and you can't answer it without digging.

Not because you're disorganized. Because the data lives in 4 different places and nobody built you a single view.

What Changes

You open one screen. You see it all. Revenue trending. Cash position clear. Team loaded or underloaded. Pipeline moving or stalled.

No digging. No spreadsheet gymnastics. No guessing. Just clarity, at a glance, every time.

Built for owners who think in questions

"Can I afford to hire?"

Current cash, projected cash, and payroll load — the answer is obvious before you ask it.

"Are we actually profitable?"

Revenue vs. expense, margin by service line, and trailing trends — no P&L required.

"Why does growth feel harder?"

Utilization and capacity data show where your team is stretched and where you're leaving money.

"What's coming next?"

Pipeline tracking with weighted revenue gives you forward visibility. Planning, not reacting.
