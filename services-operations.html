Operations & People

Numbers don't mean anything without the right people and processes to execute. We help you build both.

Fix Your Operations

Hiring & Staffing Strategy

Hiring is expensive. Hiring wrong is more expensive. Before you post that job ad, let's make sure you're hiring the right role at the right time.

We help you figure out: Do you actually need a full-time employee, or would a contractor work? What should you pay to get the person you need? How do you structure the role so they can actually succeed?

And yes, we can help with the actual hiring too - job descriptions, interview processes, offer letters, onboarding. The whole thing.

Process Documentation & Training

If everything lives in your head, your business can't grow. At some point you need systems that other people can follow.

We help you document what you do, how you do it, and why - in a way that's actually useful. Not a 50-page manual nobody reads. Clear, practical documentation that lets you delegate without disaster.

We also build training programs so new people can get up to speed without you spending weeks hand-holding.

Scalable Systems Design

The systems that worked when you had 3 employees break when you have 10. The process that was fine at $500K revenue falls apart at $2M.

We help you design operations that can grow with you. Scheduling systems, communication workflows, job tracking, invoicing processes - whatever's breaking, we can help fix it.

Sometimes that means new software. Sometimes it means using what you have differently. We'll figure out what actually makes sense for your situation.

Gap Analysis & Optimization

Something's not working but you're not sure what. Revenue is up but profit is down. You're busier than ever but feeling less in control.

We dig in and find the gaps - where work is falling through the cracks, where money is leaking out, where people are wasting time on things that don't matter.

Then we help you fix it. Not with a fancy report you'll never look at again, but with specific changes you can implement.

Operations work is often where the real problems are. Most clients come to us thinking they have a "books problem" or a "money problem." Usually it's an operations problem that shows up in the books. We can help with both.
