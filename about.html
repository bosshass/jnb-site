Hi, I'm Sara.

JNB started because I kept meeting business owners who were intimidated by their own numbers - and they had no reason to be. They just needed someone to explain things in a way that made sense.

Some clients need a full financial transformation. Some just need someone to clean up QuickBooks so they can file taxes. I do both.

What I don't do is make you feel bad for not knowing this stuff already. Numbers didn't come naturally to me either - I figured it out because I had to, and now I help other people do the same.

You're great at your trade. That's why you started your business. But being the best electrician, consultant, or security tech doesn't automatically make you great at running a company - and nobody teaches you that part. Most clients come to me with a "books problem" and discover it goes a lot deeper.

The real story

I barely passed accounting in high school. Needed an abacus to graduate - that's not a joke. Numbers were not my thing.

But somewhere along the way, I realized the problem wasn't that I couldn't do math. The problem was that nobody had ever explained financial concepts in a way that made sense to me. Once I figured it out for myself, I started helping other people figure it out too.

That's still what I do. I translate. I take the spreadsheets and reports and accounting jargon and turn it into plain English that business owners can actually use to make decisions.

Most accountants stop at the report. Most entrepreneurs stop at instinct. JNB was built to fill the gap between those two worlds - connecting the story in your numbers to the strategy in your mind. We speak human first, numbers second.

These days I work with a small team - just a handful of us, but we cover a lot of ground. Bookkeeping, fractional CFO work, operations consulting, even web and app development when that's what a client needs. We're based in Littleton, Colorado, but we work with clients all over.

The common thread is always the same: we help you understand your business better than you did before.
