Running a business is heavy.

Even when revenue is coming in. Even when things look fine from the outside.

I'm Sara.

I built JNB LLC around one principle: financial clarity should change how you run your business — not just satisfy compliance.

I've worked directly inside growing businesses — cleaning up messy books, restructuring reporting, and helping owners finally understand what their numbers were actually saying.

What I saw over and over again wasn't incompetence. It was noise.

What Changes When You Work With Us

First, the noise settles. Your books get clean. Your reports make sense. Your taxes stop feeling like a looming threat.

But more importantly — you start to see clearly. You understand where money is going. You recognize patterns. You see pressure points before they become problems.

That shift is powerful. Because clarity doesn't just improve your numbers. It changes how you feel running your business.

What We Deliver

Consistent Reporting

Monthly financials delivered on time, every time. Clear, organized, and easy to read — no accounting jargon.

In-House Tax Prep

Business taxes prepared by the same team that manages your books. No handoff confusion. No scrambling at year-end.

Financial Visibility

You'll know where revenue stands, what expenses are doing, and what cash actually looks like. No fluff. No guessing.

Strategic Insight

When you're ready for it — cash flow forecasting, margin analysis, hiring models, and financial scenario planning.

Who This Is For

Business owners who care. The ones who think about payroll before they fall asleep. Who worry about hiring too soon — or too late. Who want to grow, but don't want chaos to come with it.

You don't need hype. You don't need pressure. You need clarity.

Why Clients Stay

Because clarity compounds.

When your bookkeeping is stable and taxes are predictable, you stop operating in reaction mode. When your financial strategy is intentional, decisions feel lighter. When your systems are structured, time frees up.

This is not about "doing accounting." It's about reducing friction inside your business.

How We Grow With You

Most clients start with bookkeeping and tax support. As things stabilize, the questions get bigger.
