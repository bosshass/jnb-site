Your Numbers Are Lying to You

Amazon's reports are designed for Amazon — not for you. Between FBA fees, storage costs, returns, advertising spend, and multi-channel complexity, most sellers have no idea what their actual margins are at the product level.

You might think a product is profitable because it sells well. But after fees, returns, and ad spend? It could be draining your business.

What We Do

  • Amazon Seller Bookkeeping — Reconcile settlements, track fees, and keep your books clean across all channels.
  • SKU-Level Profitability — Know exactly which products make money and which ones are quietly killing your margins.
  • FBA Fee Analysis & Optimization — Break down every fee Amazon charges and find opportunities to reduce costs.
  • Wholesale Margin Tracking — Track true landed costs, vendor performance, and margin trends across your catalog.
  • Multi-Currency & Global Accounting — Region-specific P&L, exchange rate tracking, and country-specific fee analysis for global sellers.

Who This Is For

Amazon sellers, wholesale distributors, and multi-channel ecommerce businesses who've outgrown spreadsheet-based tracking. You're generating revenue but you can't tell which products, channels, or markets are actually profitable. You want clarity — not more reports.
